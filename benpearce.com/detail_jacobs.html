JACOBS // JACOBITES ELECTION


3 x 90 second animations to promote Jacobs new JacoBites snack. In the 'JacoBites Election' campaign, consumers are invited to vote for their favourite flavour with the potential reward of winning a holiday to the destination of their chosen flavour. The animations are electoral broadcasts for each of the 3 candidate flavours and were shown in various promotional events around the country.
