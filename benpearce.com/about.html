I'm a freelance illustrator, animator and art director specialising in motion graphics and 2D character animation. I'm currently based in East London. I previously spent several years as a design director at London creative agency Isobar working on digital projects for a wide range of high profile clients including Google, Adidas, Reebok, Magners, Kellogg's, Virgin, 3, Nokia, T Mobile, Skype and Toyota.

Since going freelance I've worked with some fantastic production houses and agencies such as Stink, Partizan, Work Club, Analog Folk, Dare, Lean Mean Fighting Machine and Razorfish to name a few.

I'm always keen for new projects and collaborations, so please do get in touch
