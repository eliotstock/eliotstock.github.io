HARK


Series of 4 posters for an upcoming exhibition by Hark for Jaguarshoes Collective. Featuring designs by Audrey Roger and Andrew MacGregor, the posters can be cut out and folded to make various animal totems. The show will run from 26th January until 26th March at The Old Shoreditch Station. Posters for sale at the No One shop.
